Questions, Answered

How far in advance should I plan my trip?

We recommend reaching out 4–6 weeks before travel for optimal availability, though we've arranged seamless VIP experiences in as little as 10 days. Early planning ensures access to premier accommodations, exclusive experiences, and preferred routing with fast track services.

Can I make changes after booking?

Absolutely. Your itinerary remains flexible throughout the planning process. We understand priorities shift—whether adjusting dates, upgrading cabin class, or adding destinations. Our concierge team handles modifications seamlessly, keeping you informed of any fare differences or availability.

What if I want to change my destination mid-planning?

We welcome destination changes during the planning phase. Our team will present new routing options, accommodation alternatives, and curated experiences for your revised destination. This flexibility is part of our VIP service—your vision guides the journey.

Do you provide support during my trip?

Yes. Our 24/7 concierge line remains active throughout your journey. From flight delays to last-minute restaurant reservations, we're available via WhatsApp, phone, or email. Think of us as your travel command center—always one message away.

What information do you need to start planning?

Just the essentials: preferred travel dates (or flexible windows), destination interests, passenger count, and any specific requests like kosher meals or accessibility needs. From there, we'll craft a preliminary itinerary and refine it together until every detail aligns with your expectations.

What makes this a VIP experience?

Exclusive access. We secure priority boarding, fast track immigration clearance, lounge access with premium carriers like American Airlines and El Al, and direct connections to luxury ground transportation. Every touchpoint is designed to feel effortless—no queues, no friction, just seamless travel.
